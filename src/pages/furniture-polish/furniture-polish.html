Furniture Polish

The most know coconut oil benefits are skin, and health based. Coconut oil can also serve as a natural polish that can restore and give furniture a new shine.

Regular furniture polish is highly toxic and can harm your health. It contains hydrocarbons and phenols which are detrimental to the well-being of any individual. Phenols are carcinogenic, and nitrobenzene another component can penetrate your skin. Once it reaches your vital organs, it can cause irreparable damage.

Among the coconut oil benefits is its low price, and once you buy it, you can use in for many household uses. The most popular application is its use as a wood polish after mixing with lemon juice. It will also smell better and is less toxic.

Coconut Oil Benefits on Your Leather Furniture

As you replace the toxic shine with a natural product, it will sink quicker in wood grains. The leather furniture will last longer and ensures that no matter the age of the furniture it looks new. When applying the pure coconut oil, the leather furniture must be cleaned to ensure that there is dust layer on the surface. You can rub some coconut oil the old leather furniture to keep it looking at its best.

Recuperates Wood Paint

Old furniture needs occasional maintenance to restore the initial appealing look. If you feel like the wooden furniture deserves a new fresh coat of varnish, achieve this using coconut oil.

With a small amount of the oil, dapple on the ancient wooden piece, and use a piece of cloth spread the oil. You will start noticing the color turning into a deeper hue as the wood absorbs the oil. The oil adds a new layer of coating on the oil that can prevent future damage to the wood.

Making the Leather Glossy

Patent leather furniture is meant to be glossy and varnished. Over time this shiny surface can disappear, and develop scratches. Coconut can make the scratches less notable and restore the original glossy appearance. The alternatives are alcohol and shoe polish, which are toxic and smell bad.

Lubrication of Hinges of Furniture

One of the fascinating coconut oil benefits is its ability to lubricate, moving parts, and machine components. The oil has similar properties as petroleum-based lubricating oils and grease. Lubricating joins is simple; scoop some coconut oil and apply to hinges. Turn the moving parts severally for the oil to distribute. You can do this for scissors, doors, windows, or for fans.
