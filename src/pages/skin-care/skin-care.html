Skin Care

People are waking up to the realization of the potential coconut oil benefits for the skin. For the past few years, it has become increasingly popular, and more brands have started blending it in different skin care products. It derives its usefulness from the nourishing active composites.

Top Coconut Oil Benefits for Skin Care

Anti-aging Cream

It is a useful property that makes it highly sought and used in natural creams. Antioxidants present in the oil, repair and replenish dry skin. They also improve the appearance of wrinkles effectively slowing down the aging process. It has hydration properties which reduce skin dryness and keeps the skin layers firm to reduce creases and prevent scarring.

When epidermis absorbs coconut oil, it can give you a glowing skin and prevent damage to cells in the dermis which produce collagen.

Lip Gel

Cracked lips can make you feel uncomfortable, embarrassed and worried. This condition results when people fail to realize that the skin covering the lips is sensitive, often requiring nourishing and natural exfoliation.

Coconut oil is excellent for lip gel since it shields the lips from temperature changes and environmental effects such as dust and UV rays. The oil is edible and natural, even if you swallow some of it; you are still safe. Apart from being protective, it gives your lips, plumpness and a bit of shine.

Skin Cleanser

Coconut oil benefits to your skin are numerous. It can reduce the production of sebum (oily secretions) and remove impurities in the pores. Using it for cleansing will leave your skin looking flawless and radiant. The oil softens the keratin caps, which build up in the pores. It dissolves hard sebum and is perfect for acne prone skin due to its anti-inflammatory properties.

For better results when using the oil for cleansing, you can add exfoliating elements like sugar and salt. Mixing the two products can provide an all-natural face and body scrub. Cleansing yourself with this combination will leave your skin with closed pores, an even-color, and a soft texture.

Bacterial and Fungal Treatment

Anti-inflammatory properties of the oil can also benefit your skin in more ways than you can imagine.

Fungal infections such as Tinea pedis and Athletes foot are curable when using coconut oil. This can be attributed to the high concentration of lauric acid in the oil which is a powerful anti-bacterial and a pathogen killer.
